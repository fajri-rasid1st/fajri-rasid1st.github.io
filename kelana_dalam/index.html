01 - Mission Expedition

Seeing and researching the native flora and fauna of South Sulawesi

The Sulawesi lowland rain forests is an ecoregion, within the tropical and subtropical moist broadleaf forests biome, of the large island of Sulawesi in Southeast Asia. It supports approximately 15.000 plant species, 380 bird species, and several mammal species.
